About

Sam Bodhi

Born in Green Bay WI, his family moved to Kansas City and then to Chicago when he was 10 years old, Sam Bodhi grew up there in Chicago where he attended Roosevelt High School and Northeastern Illinois University where he studied music theory and classical guitar. Growing up and as a young adult in Chicago he was exposed to blues and jazz. After leaving school he changed instruments to piano. Stylistically, in public performance, urban blues dominated the repertoire which initially, when changing instruments he stuck with and taught himself the blues on piano. He toured/performed blues across the United States and in Europe. Bodhi was involved with the music scene in Los Angeles where he lived 4 years playing at clubs on Sunset Blvd and Santa Monica Blvd including the Troubadour Club. After leaving L.A. and expanding into jazz he found himself in New Orleans developing his piano style and jazz repertoire. Since returning to Green Bay from New Orleans, Bodhi has been entertaining at nightspots in northeastern Wisconsin.
